Your 100% Private, Fast, and Powerful Image Tool

Welcome to the easiest way to resize, compress, and convert images. Our tool is built on three key principles:

  • 1. Total Privacy: Your images never leave your computer. There are no uploads, no servers, and no data collection. All processing happens 100% locally in your browser, making it the safest way to handle sensitive photos or documents.
  • 2. Powerful Control: Quickly change formats (like JPG, PNG, and modern WEBP), resize by pixels, and compress files to hit an exact target size.
  • 3. Instant Speed: Since there are no uploads, every operation is instant. Convert or resize a 10MB photo in less than a second.

How to Use the Tool

Step 1: Upload or Drop Your Image

Drag your file onto the drop zone, or click to select it. The tool runs entirely in your browser, so your image opens instantly with zero upload time.

Step 2: Resize, Rotate, or Flip

Need a specific size? Enter a new width or height in pixels (px). Keep "Maintain Ratio" checked (recommended) to prevent stretching or squashing your image. You can also use the buttons to quickly rotate or flip your image.

Step 3: Choose Format & Compression

This is where the magic happens. Select your output format (e.g., convert PNG to JPG, or JPG to the highly-efficient WEBP).

Next, choose your **Compression Mode**:

  • Quality: (Recommended for most uses). This lets you visually control the compression. A 90% quality JPEG or 85% WEBP looks fantastic and is a fraction of the original size.
  • Target Size (KB): (Perfect for forms). Need a photo under 100KB for a government site? Just select this mode, type "100", and the tool will automatically adjust the quality to hit that exact file size.

Step 4: Download Your New Image

Click "Resize & Download." Your new, optimized image is processed and saved directly to your computer. That's it!
